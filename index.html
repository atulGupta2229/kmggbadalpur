Message From the Principal

Dear All,

Km.Mayawati Government Girls Polytechnic, Badalpur, established in 2002 is one of the best Polytechnic in U.P. and located at Badalpur (G.B.Nagar). Considered as UP’s one of the leading Government Polytechnic, we aim at providing skill based diploma education to students’ through state-of-art curriculum designed in co-ordination with industry. It is framed with a view of transforming students, who could be of tremendous help in making meaningful contributions to industries, community and rural development as well as inculcating a spirit of self-employment through all round training and skill development courses. This institute has a wealth of talent and knowledge with qualified faculty and high merit students. It is equipped with good physical facilities in the form of buildings, lecture halls, laboratories, workshops, hostels etc. We consistently adapt to the changing needs through continuous up gradation of all our resources. My organization is committed towards imparting education which as defined by Swami Vivekananda says “Education should be man-making, life giving and character building”.

Thank You
S.N.Singh
Principal, KMGGP (Badalpur)
