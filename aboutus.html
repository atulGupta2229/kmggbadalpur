About the College

Km. Mayawati Government Girls Polytechnic, Badalpur was established in the year 2002. It is an institute of technical education of Uttar Pradesh in India. The present campus houses in its 7.3 acre campus, a part of which is covered by a main building, hostel buildings, mechanical workshop, virtual class room, well furnished library and classrooms for various faculties including Electronics Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Information Technology and other facilities and has the capacity to groom more than 180 students in various engineering fields every year. Over one decade, the institute has produced more than 2000 diploma engineers in different disciplines, has won several awards in academics as well as socio-cultural activities and on the whole has succeeded in contributing humble positive efforts towards the building of a progressive society.

Vision

"To transform each and every student as successful industry professional by bridging the gap Between academia and industries"

Mission

"To impart Industry Oriented Technical Education to the students through Excellent Teaching and Learning, timely interaction with Industries and to yield better job opportunities. "
